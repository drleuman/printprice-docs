# Cost Optimization

Cost Optimization is one of the strategic functions of the PrintPrice platform.

It ensures that pricing and routing are not only technically valid, but also economically efficient.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of cost optimization is to identify the most competitive manufacturing path for a print job.

This may include:

- selecting the best printer
- adjusting manufacturing assumptions
- comparing cost structures
- reducing operational friction

---

## Sources of Optimization

Optimization may come from several sources:

### Printer Selection
Choosing the best partner based on pricing and compatibility.

### Format Efficiency
Aligning the document with more efficient production configurations.

### Manufacturing Constraints
Avoiding technically risky setups that increase cost indirectly.

### Network Effects
Using a broader printer network to improve cost competitiveness.

---

## Strategic Importance

Cost optimization makes PrintPrice more than a calculator.

It turns the platform into an intelligent decision system capable of improving production outcomes at scale.
