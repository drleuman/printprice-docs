# Product Roadmap

The PrintPrice product roadmap describes the strategic evolution of the platform from pricing utility to production intelligence system.

It reflects both product maturity and operational ambition.

---

## Current Foundation

The platform already brings together several important layers:

- production pricing
- PDF technical analysis
- manufacturing feasibility
- printer compatibility evaluation
- automated production routing

This foundation differentiates PrintPrice from simpler quoting tools.

---

## Near-Term Priorities

Near-term roadmap themes may include:

- stronger documentation coverage
- richer partner capability profiles
- more explainable routing decisions
- better assistant-guided workflows
- improved admin visibility
- tighter integration contracts

These improvements strengthen both usability and platform trust.

---

## Mid-Term Priorities

Mid-term development may focus on:

- partner onboarding workflows
- more advanced preflight intelligence
- routing policy controls
- production order automation
- richer compatibility scoring
- operational dashboards

This phase moves the platform closer to manufacturing orchestration.

---

## Long-Term Direction

Long-term roadmap areas may include:

- distributed print network coordination
- logistics-aware decision making
- sustainability-aware routing
- capacity-aware partner selection
- automated tender workflows
- cross-partner production intelligence

At this stage, PrintPrice becomes a coordination layer rather than only a calculator.

---

## Strategic Framing

The roadmap is not just about adding features.

It is about deepening the platform's role in the print value chain by increasing:

- intelligence
- automation
- explainability
- operational leverage

---

## Product Philosophy

The roadmap should remain aligned with a few core principles:

- deterministic where reliability matters
- explainable where trust matters
- modular where scale matters
- automation-first where workflows are repetitive

These principles help maintain coherence as the system expands.

---

## Outcome

If the roadmap is executed well, PrintPrice evolves into a central production intelligence platform for publishers, print partners, and manufacturing coordinators.
